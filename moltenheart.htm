    IN SUMMARY - Robert Smith?
     I really enjoyed this! I love that it's one of the very few NDAs not to be an alien invasion cliche. Instead, it has a sense of wonder, worldbuilding and deals with an analogue of climate change, only featuring rock people living beneath a planet. There's no real villain, only a misguided leader who's eventually put right, and there's charm aplenty. More like this, please.
