    BASIC PLOT
     The TARDIS lands in the middle of the American Civil War. With the Doctor trapped on the other side of the conflict, Peri and Erimem must struggle to survive the country's racism and hatred.

    IN SUMMARY - Robert Smith?
     Fantastic. One of the shortest novellas, but thoroughly involving. Presenting the events through letters and various extracts really makes this book, and for once you feel for the original characters even more than the TARDIS crew. Of the regulars, Peri gets the best work, although the Doctor is reasonably done. Erimem is almost nonexistent, which is a shame for those of us new to her, but probably unavoidable given the subject matter. Which is thoroughly involving and magnificently portrayed. Deliciously done.
