Hope
by Mark Clapham

Publisher: BBC
ISBN: 0 563 53846 5

     

    BASIC PLOT
     The city of Hope is an environmental disaster. The air is thick with fog, law and order no longer exist - and the TARDIS is trapped below the toxic sea. While the Doctor hunts for a killer, the most powerful man on the planet is making a very dangerous offer.

    DOCTOR
     Eighth.

    COMPANIONS
     Fitz and Anji.

    MATERIALISATION CIRCUIT
     Ice below the city of Hope, the planet Endpoint.

    PREPARATORY READING
     The Adventuress of Henrietta Street and a basic knowledge of what happens at the end of Escape Velocity.

    CONTINUITY REFERENCES
     Pg 7 "While once two hearts pumped within the Doctor's chest, now only a single pulse beats beneath his ribs." We saw the Doctor lose his second heart in The Adventuress of Henrietta Street.

    "As he thinks, he rubs his newly shaven chin" The Doctor wore a beard for the past two stories.

    Pg 8 "Or at least a better, safer place to wake up than a storm-lashed wasteland in the path of a city-machine or in the attic room where an Ogre's mother had made dresses for several ugly sisters" Dark Progeny and Grimm Reality.

    Pg 35 "She recognised a poodle's woollen bootie, [...] what looked like a diamond engagement ring" Mad Dogs and Englishmen, The Adventuress of Henrietta Street.

    Pg 132 "There are signs of surgery around the chest area" The Adventuress of Henrietta Street.

    Pg 192 "'When you put it like that, it is odd,' said Anji. 'To be in the same room as your entire species.'" The Ark in Space.

    The planet A2756, a graveyard planet (see page 241).

    IN SUMMARY - Robert Smith?
     It's a bit clumsy, plotwise, but it works nonetheless. It's nice to see some examination of the current state of affairs, with a strong role for the Doctor and an amazing examination of Anji. The story is split oddly in half, with little attempt to integrate the two plotlines, but it's definitely worth your time. Not the brilliant novel it might have been, but still very good.
