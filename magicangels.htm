    IN SUMMARY - Robert Smith?
     It's a cute little read, as you might expect. The Angels hardly appear, which is probably for the best... and then the climax is basically a retread of Blink (two Angels staring at each other for the rest of time) by way of an image of an Angel becoming an Angel from The Time of Angels along the way. The Doctor's a bit too much of an idiot in the early pages, both with the inept and desperately unfunny donut eating, as well as the irritating namedropping. Fortunately, after that it settles down nicely.
